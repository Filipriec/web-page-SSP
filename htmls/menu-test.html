Sci-hub & access to a scientific research

In the video I step by step explain how to find and access any research paper.
For searching articles I use Google scholar or any library. I recommend checking journals e.g. aiaa
As library I use z-lib or ...
To access any locked reserach paper, just copy the DOI and paste it into the sci-hub. You can find sci-hub on any search engine.
